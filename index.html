I am a Ph.D. student at Erik Jonsson School of Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas. I’m advised by Prof. Vincent Ng and often collaborate with Yufang Hou. My research interests are centered around natural language processing and machine learning, particularly on bridging anaphora resolution, coreference resolution, and applying them to downstream tasks.

Interests: Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning.
